Apple's Reimagined Siri Arrives — Powered by Google's Gemini

Apple officially announced a completely reimagined, AI-powered Siri debuting with iOS 26.4 in March 2026. In a surprising partnership, Apple is leveraging Google's 1.2 trillion parameter Gemini AI model, running on Apple's Private Cloud Compute infrastructure to maintain strict privacy standards.

The update follows rigorous internal testing to meet Apple's reliability benchmarks. This marks a significant shift in Apple's AI strategy, acknowledging the need for external AI expertise while maintaining its privacy-first approach.
