The CLARITY Act's floor path remains intact following Tuesday's ethics provision acceptance, with floor amendment language finalization now the outstanding procedural step and 16 days remaining before Senate recess begins August 7. Polymarket passage odds at 43% reflect the tight timeline rather than deteriorating political prospects. Bitcoin spot ETFs ended a 10-day outflow streak on Wednesday, with institutional buyers returning as the floor path became clearer — even as the oil-driven BTC pullback continued in the spot market.

The 43% Polymarket number deserves specific interpretation. It does not mean passage is less likely than failure on the merits — it reflects the probability that passage occurs before August 7, the hard deadline. If the floor amendment language takes the week of July 21 to finalize rather than hours, the July 28 window becomes the operative one — meaning the odds priced into Polymarket include scenarios where passage is politically achievable but procedurally misses the recess. Senate Majority Leader Thune's commitment to a floor vote before recess is the variable that prevents a procedural miss from becoming a political one. The 10-day Bitcoin ETF outflow streak ending on the same day as the CLARITY floor path clarified is the institutional market processing the probability-weighted outcome: 43% passage before August 7 is worth buying on a pullback driven by oil, not CLARITY deterioration.

Quick hits

  • The CLARITY ethics language scope question has not been resolved publicly: Trump's acceptance of "the ethics provision" on Tuesday covered crypto income disclosure requirements for administration officials, but the specific text — all administration officials vs. senior financial officials only vs. senior officials with public disclosure — determines whether Senators Murphy, Van Hollen, and Merkley's floor votes shift from no to yes. They named "the ethics provision" as their price; the administration accepted "the ethics provision." Whether their definition and the administration's definition match is what floor amendment finalization reveals. If the language is narrower than what the three Senators described as their demand, they retain the option to maintain their opposition on the grounds that the final text doesn't match the commitment. That is the remaining political variable in the CLARITY floor timeline.
  • New Hampshire's crypto law follows a state-level pattern that Wyoming established in 2019 and Texas, Arizona, and Florida have since extended. The significance of the accumulation is not any single state's law — it is that Bitcoin and crypto companies now have four to five favorable state jurisdictions for legal operations, corporate domicile, and mining activity. This matters to the CLARITY Act's urgency calculation: with functional state-level frameworks available, the immediate operational pressure for a federal framework is lower than it was in 2023 when Wyoming was the only favorable jurisdiction. State laws create a floor; CLARITY creates a ceiling. Favorable states reduce the cost of a CLARITY delay; they don't eliminate the value of a CLARITY passage for market structure clarity at the national level.
