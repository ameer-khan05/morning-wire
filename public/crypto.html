The Anchor

BTC stalls below $76K as traders wait on proof, not promises.

The geopolitical relief rally that carried Bitcoin back above $75,000 this week has run into a wall of overhead supply. CoinDesk puts the live sell-order stack at roughly $450 million between spot and $76K — enough to stall every upside attempt of the last 48 hours. Derivatives desks are quietly adding downside hedges.

The bigger picture is the one the tape keeps whispering: Bitcoin is still 43% off the October 2025 all-time high near $126,000. Ceasefires rally it. But what it's really waiting for is a Fed pivot — and March CPI at 3.3% didn't help.
