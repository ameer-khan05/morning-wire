BIP-360 · Quantum April 29 Nakamoto Stage panel · Bitcoin Core merged · testnet v0.3.0

BIP-360 quantum-resistant wallet panel at Bitcoin 2026 — the upgrade protecting $6.9M BTC from future quantum decryption.

A panel titled "How Real Is The Quantum Threat?" takes place April 29 on the Nakamoto Stage at Bitcoin 2026 in Las Vegas, featuring Hunter Beast — co-author of BIP-360, the quantum-resistant Bitcoin wallet proposal already merged into the Bitcoin Core BIP repository in February 2026. BIP-360 introduces a new P2MR (Pay to Merkle Root) address type via a soft fork that removes "key path spending" — the feature that directly exposes a user's public key during transactions — replacing it with Merkle tree and SHA-256 hash-based verification that is not vulnerable to quantum factoring attacks. CoinDesk reported this week that approximately 6.9 million BTC — including coins in Satoshi Nakamoto's original wallets — could theoretically be drained by a sufficiently powerful quantum computer using the current ECDSA signature scheme. BIP-360 was deployed on Bitcoin Quantum Testnet v0.3.0 in March; network-wide activation requires the Bitcoin community's consensus process.

Kevin Warsh · Fed Chair Apr 29 Senate vote · Tillis clears · May 15 Powell term end

Kevin Warsh Federal Reserve chair confirmation vote April 29 — path cleared; crypto regulatory implications of a new Fed begin.

The Senate Banking Committee is scheduled to vote on Kevin Warsh's confirmation as Federal Reserve chair on Wednesday April 29, following Senator Thom Tillis's announcement Sunday that he is dropping his blockade of the nomination. With Tillis's support, the committee passes 13-11 on party lines; a full Senate vote could confirm Warsh before Powell's term ends May 15. For crypto markets, the Warsh confirmation is significant on two levels: first, Warsh has expressed more openness to digital-asset regulation and clearer market-structure rules than the Powell-era Fed; second, the monetary policy transition lands at a sensitive moment — the FOMC meeting is May 7, the April PCE inflation print is May 1, and Brent is above $108. Warsh vowed in his April 21 confirmation hearing not to be Trump's "sock puppet" on rates — markets will test that statement within weeks of confirmation as the inflation and energy data comes in.

CLARITY Act Senate markup window now days away — Lummis warns missing it delays crypto market structure to 2030.

Senate Banking Committee Chair Tim Scott is expected to hold a markup session for the Digital Asset Market Clarity Act before the end of April — a window that is now days away and may be crowded out by the Warsh confirmation vote and Big Tech earnings absorption. Senator Lummis has publicly warned that missing this markup window could push comprehensive US digital-asset market-structure legislation to 2030 as the congressional calendar fills. The CLARITY Act would resolve stablecoin yield ambiguity from the GENIUS Act's Section 4(c), establish a DeFi regulatory framework, and clarify exchange licensing. Tether's USDT supply has grown to nearly $150 billion — providing the liquidity base for the broader crypto market. The GENIUS Act stablecoin implementing regulations are due by July 18, 2026 — 82 days away — giving the CLARITY Act limited time to integrate with the stablecoin framework before the regulatory clock runs out.

Iran's Hormuz proposal briefly spiked BTC above $79K — oil at $108 capped the move as macro uncertainty returned.

Bitcoin's intraday move to $79,000 Monday was directly triggered by the morning report of Iran's Hormuz reopening proposal — a signal that risk assets across equities, crypto, and commodities responded to as a potential end to the oil supply shock. The gain quickly reversed as markets absorbed the details: the proposal defers nuclear talks indefinitely, makes no nuclear concessions, and shows no US acceptance signal. Brent surging above $108 on the same day reinforced that the Hormuz premium is not going away. BTC settled near $78,670, up 1.49% on the day. Veteran trader Peter Brandt's forecast of an "investable low" in September-October 2026 remains the dominant medium-term cycle model; at current prices, BTC is trading approximately 38–48% below Cantor Fitzgerald's $212K and Bernstein's $150K year-end targets — a discount that implies significant post-cycle appreciation if the models hold.

Quick hits

  • The attendance of US Attorney General Todd Blanche and FBI Director Kash Patel at Bitcoin 2026 as headliners — rather than enforcers — marks a complete reversal of the federal government's posture toward Bitcoin developers since Operation Chokepoint 2.0. The "Code is Free Speech" framing is a direct rebuttal to the DOJ's prosecution of Tornado Cash and Samourai Wallet developers in 2023–2024.
  • Strategy's 108-purchase accumulation strategy has effectively removed approximately 818,334 BTC from circulating supply — roughly 4.1% of the total 21 million BTC cap — creating a structural supply constraint that every new purchase reinforces. The company now holds more BTC than any single nation except the US government.
  • BIP-360's quantum threat is not theoretical: Google's Willow quantum chip, announced in late 2025, demonstrated 10^15 operations against a benchmark problem in under 5 minutes — still orders of magnitude below what is needed to crack ECDSA in practice, but the trajectory has shortened the Bitcoin community's timeline for response from "decades away" to "within this decade."
  • The Warsh confirmation's crypto implications extend beyond monetary policy: a new Fed chair will set the tone for the Federal Reserve's response to stablecoin reserve requirements, CBDC development, and the bank regulatory framework for digital-asset custody — all of which are live rule-writing processes in 2026.
  • Tether's $150 billion USDT supply means the stablecoin has more assets than the sovereign wealth funds of most G20 nations; its reserve management — primarily US Treasury bills — makes Tether one of the 20 largest buyers of short-dated US government debt globally, a fact with direct implications for Treasury market liquidity.
  • BTC dominance at 60.31% is the highest since February; historically, when BTC dominance peaks above 60%, the altcoin rally phase begins within 4–8 weeks as BTC-denominated gains are rotated into higher-beta assets — a pattern that has held in the 2020 and 2024 cycles.
