Day 48 · Ceasefire Day 1 10 Lebanon truce · days in effect
The Anchor

A truce at dawn, a strait reopened, a world holding its breath.

The sounds over Beirut last night were celebratory — fireworks, car horns, gunfire fired into the air. A 10-day ceasefire between Israel and Hezbollah took effect at midnight local time, brokered by the Trump administration after the first direct talks between the sides in a generation. Hours later, Iran's foreign minister declared the Strait of Hormuz "completely open" for the duration of the truce, with commercial vessels cleared to transit in coordinated lanes. Military vessels remain barred.

The UK and France are leading a virtual summit today, gathering 40 heads of state to convert a fragile pause into something durable. Zelenskyy called it "the right decision that leads to ending the war" — and quietly pressed for a parallel Ukraine track. Trump told reporters en route to Mar-a-Lago that the wider Iran framework "should be ending pretty soon."

Middle East Day 1 Lebanon truce · overnight

The 10-day Israel–Lebanon ceasefire takes effect.

Celebratory gunfire lit up Beirut at midnight as the truce began. Brokered by the Trump administration through direct talks in Washington, it's the first bilateral Israel–Lebanon diplomatic engagement in decades.

Source: CNN
Shipping Open Strait of Hormuz

Iran declares the Strait of Hormuz "completely open."

Commercial transits are permitted for the duration of the ceasefire, subject to coordination with the Revolutionary Guard. Military vessels remain barred; lanes are restricted to Iran-approved corridors.

Source: Al Jazeera
Diplomacy 40 Nations · UK/France summit

40-nation virtual summit convenes today on Hormuz.

London and Paris co-chair the talks. The agenda: a durable shipping protocol, humanitarian access to Lebanon, and preliminary scaffolding for the broader Iran framework Trump signaled yesterday.

Source: CBS News
Ukraine Paused Trilateral talks · stalled since war

Zelenskyy seizes the moment to press for a Kyiv track.

Ukraine's president called the US–Iran ceasefire "the right decision that leads to ending the war." Trilateral Kyiv–Moscow–Washington talks have been paused since the Iran war began in early March; Zelenskyy wants them restarted within days.

Source: Wikipedia
US · Recovery "Leisurely" Trump · enriched material recovery

US to enter Iran at "leisurely pace" to recover enriched material.

Trump told reporters the US will move on a "leisurely" timetable to recover highly enriched material that was displaced during the March strikes. Inspectors from the IAEA are expected to accompany the mission.

Source: Times of Israel
